About NaijaAutos

NaijaAutos is Nigeria's leading automotive marketplace, connecting car buyers and renters with trusted dealers and private sellers since 2016. We've built our reputation on transparency, quality, and exceptional customer service.

With showrooms in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, we offer a wide selection of brand new, foreign used (Tokunbo), and Nigerian used vehicles. Our team of automotive experts is dedicated to helping you find the perfect car that fits your budget and lifestyle.
