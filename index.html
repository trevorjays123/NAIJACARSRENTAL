❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about car rentals in Nigeria

How much does it cost to rent a car in Lagos per day?

Car rental rates in Lagos vary by vehicle type:

  • • Economy cars (Toyota Camry): ₦80,000 - ₦120,000/day
  • • Executive sedans (Lexus): ₦160,000 - ₦200,000/day
  • • Luxury SUVs (Land Cruiser): ₦250,000 - ₦400,000/day
  • • Buses (Toyota Hiace): ₦150,000 - ₦200,000/day

All rates include comprehensive insurance, maintenance, and 100km daily mileage limit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • • Bank Transfer (all Nigerian banks)
  • • Cash Payment at our offices
  • • Installment plans for long-term rentals
  • • Mobile Money (Paystack, Flutterwave)
  • • Corporate accounts for businesses

All payments are secure and we provide official receipts for all transactions.

Do you offer monthly or long-term rental discounts?

Yes! Monthly rentals receive significant discounts:

  • • Economy cars: From ₦1.5M/month (save up to 30%)
  • • Executive cars: From ₦2.5M/month (save up to 25%)
  • • Luxury SUVs: From ₦4M/month (save up to 20%)

Long-term leases (3+ months) include additional benefits like free maintenance, dedicated driver, and flexible payment terms.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

Requirements for car rental in Nigeria:

  • • Valid driver's license (Nigerian or International)
  • • Government-issued ID (International passport, Voter's card, or National ID)
  • • Proof of residence (utility bill or bank statement)
  • • Security deposit (varies by vehicle type)
  • • For corporate rentals: Company ID and introduction letter

Do I need a Nigerian driver's license to rent a car?

For self-drive rentals, you need a valid driver's license:

  • • Nigerian driver's license (mandatory for Nigerian residents)
  • • International Driver's Permit (for foreign visitors)
  • • Foreign driver's license (with valid visa)

For rentals with a driver (chauffeur service), you don't need a license as our professional drivers handle all driving.

Can I rent a car without a driver (self-drive)?

Yes, self-drive rentals are available! Requirements:

  • • Minimum 2 years driving experience
  • • Valid driver's license
  • • Security deposit (₦200,000 - ₦500,000 depending on vehicle)
  • • Age requirement: 25 years and above

Self-drive rentals include comprehensive insurance and 24/7 roadside assistance.

Do you provide airport transfer services?

Yes, we offer premium airport transfer services:

  • • Lagos (MMIA): From ₦50,000 for economy, ₦80,000 for executive
  • • Abuja (NAIA): From ₦45,000 for economy, ₦70,000 for executive
  • • Port Harcourt (PHIA): From ₦40,000 for economy

Service includes: Flight tracking, 60 minutes free waiting time, meet & greet, and luggage assistance.

Do you offer car rental services in other cities?

Absolutely! We provide services across Nigeria including:

  • • Port Harcourt (GRA, Trans Amadi)
  • • Ilorin (GRA, Fate, Tanke)
  • • Benin City
  • • Warri, Asaba, Ibadan

For locations not listed, we offer nationwide delivery with our inter-state transport service.

What wedding car services do you offer?

Our wedding car packages include:

  • • Luxury vehicles: Rolls Royce, Mercedes S-Class, Lexus SUVs
  • • Professional chauffeurs in formal attire
  • • Elegant car decoration (flowers, ribbons)
  • • Red carpet service
  • • Coordinated convoy management

Packages start from ₦180,000 for 6 hours. Book at least 2 weeks in advance for best availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair:

  • • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup
  • • 50% refund for cancellations 24-48 hours before pickup
  • • No refund for cancellations within 24 hours
  • • No-show: 100% charge
  • • Early returns: No refund for unused days

What happens if I have an accident or breakdown?

We provide comprehensive support:

  • • 24/7 roadside assistance across Nigeria
  • • Replacement vehicle within 4 hours (subject to availability)
  • • Comprehensive insurance coverage for all vehicles
  • • Dedicated accident support team

In case of accident: Call our emergency line immediately. Do not leave the vehicle unattended.

Can I rent a car for inter-state travel?

Yes, inter-state travel is one of our specialties:

  • • Lagos to Abuja: Professional drivers, rest stops included
  • • Lagos to Port Harcourt: Experienced drivers familiar with routes
  • • Port Harcourt to Abuja: Comfortable SUVs and sedans available
  • • Custom routes across Nigeria

Inter-state rates include: Driver, fuel, insurance, and overnight accommodation for driver on multi-day trips.
